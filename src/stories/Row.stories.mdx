# Core concepts

The core concept is based on [bootstrap](https://getbootstrap.com/docs/5.1/layout/columns/).

# Row

The Row lets you specify column widths across 6 breakpoint sizes (`xs`, `sm`, `md`, `lg`, `xl` and `xxl`). For every breakpoint, you can specify the amount of columns that will fit next to each other. You can also specify auto to set the columns to their natural widths.

```javascript
<Container>
    <Row xs={2} md={4} lg={6}>
        <Col>1 of 2</Col>
        <Col>2 of 2</Col>
    </Row>
    <Row xs={1} md={2}>
        <Col>1 of 3</Col>
        <Col>2 of 3</Col>
        <Col>3 of 3</Col>
    </Row>
    <Row xs="auto">
        <Col>1 of 3</Col>
        <Col>2 of 3</Col>
        <Col>3 of 3</Col>
    </Row>
</Container>
```
